Reflection

The focus of my career thus far has been information and records management, both of which I’m absolutely passionate about. I love information and ensuring that the proper information is disseminated to the right people. I love records and the history they hold which is what has inspired me to finish my Bachelor’s degree and go on for Master’s in library and information science.
Currently, I love my position and what I’m doing as I get my hands on historical records every single day. I get to know the history of the Public Works Department for the City of Boise and that gives me a great deal of knowledge. I’m lucky to be able to say that I love what I do.
In the future, my hope is to stay with the City of Boise but move departments and work for one of our many libraries. I’d love to work with children, which is why I’m getting my Bachelor’s degree in education. I also have a passion for teaching, whether it’s children or adults.
The culmination of my current field and past experience plus multiple degrees should result in the ability to obtain a new, dream position of working with kids at a local library or school.
